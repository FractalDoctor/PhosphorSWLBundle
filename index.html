△ PHOSPHOR SWL SUITE

The Complete Shortwave Radio Toolkit for iPhone.

Seven specialist utilities with a retro phosphor CRT display, built for amateur radio operators and shortwave listeners. Each tool does one thing well. Buy the suite and save compared to purchasing individually.

INCLUDED APPS

  • PhosphorRTTY — Real-time RTTY signal decoder. Shortwave radio teletype via your iPhone microphone. Baudot 45.45 baud with real-time waterfall display.
  • PhosphorCW — Real-time Morse code decoder. CW signal detection via your iPhone microphone with automatic frequency and speed tracking.
  • PhosphorSSTV — Slow Scan Television encoder and decoder. Encode or decode SSTV images via your iPhone microphone — ten modes including Scottie, Martin, and Robot.
  • PhosphorHertz — Real-time HF radio propagation monitor. NOAA space weather data translated into band-by-band conditions. Check which bands are open before you transmit.
  • PhosphorCalc — Ten RF and antenna calculators. Dipole lengths, path loss, SWR, link budgets, impedance matching. Works offline.
  • PhantomGram — Spectrum painting for amateur radio. Transmit and decode images painted onto SDR waterfall displays.
  • PhosphorSWL — Shortwave broadcast schedule browser and numbers station reference. EiBi database, scanner, and Cold War SIGINT aesthetic.
