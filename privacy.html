△ PHOSPHOR SWL SUITE — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 2026

OVERVIEW

The Phosphor SWL Suite is a bundle of seven iPhone apps. This privacy policy covers the bundle as a whole. Each individual app also has its own privacy policy, linked from its support page.

DATA COLLECTION

None of the apps in the Phosphor SWL Suite collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No app requires an account, registration, or sign-in of any kind.

MICROPHONE ACCESS

PhosphorRTTY, PhosphorCW, PhosphorSSTV, and PhantomGram use your device's microphone to capture audio for real-time signal decoding. All audio processing occurs entirely on-device in real time. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted. No audio data ever leaves your device.

NETWORK REQUESTS

PhosphorHertz and PhosphorSWL fetch publicly available data (NOAA space weather and EiBi broadcast schedules respectively) to provide their core functionality. No personal data is sent with these requests. PhosphorRTTY, PhosphorCW, PhosphorSSTV, PhosphorCalc, and PhantomGram make no network requests of any kind.

LOCAL DATA STORAGE

All apps store user preferences and session data locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and contains no personal information.

ANALYTICS AND TRACKING

No app in the suite includes any analytics frameworks, crash reporters, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking of any kind.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

No app in the suite uses third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks. All apps are built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

No app in the suite collects data from anyone, including children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date.

CONTACT

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the Phosphor SWL Suite GitHub repository.
